MAPEH?

MAPEH is a school subject that stands for Music, Arts, Physical Education, and Health. It is a comprehensive subject that combines various areas of learning to promote the holistic development of students. Each component of MAPEH serves a specific purpose, contributing to the overall growth and well-being of learners.
Pick the Section you want to learn
Music
This teaches students about sounds, songs, and instruments. It’s all about enjoying and understanding different kinds of music, from singing to playing instruments.
Arts
This is where students learn to express themselves through drawing, painting, crafting, or other creative activities. It’s like unlocking your inner artist!
Physical Education
This involves physical activities, like sports, games, and exercises. It’s designed to keep students active and teach them the importance of staying fit.
Health
This part focuses on teaching students about their bodies, proper nutrition, hygiene, and how to take care of their overall well-being.
